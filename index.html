Nevada is a state in the Western region of the United States. Nevada is the 7th-most extensive, the 32nd-most populous, and the 9th-least densely populated of the U.S. states

Historical Facts

Nevada is the historical homeland of many Native American Tribes. Nevada was a Battle Born State and the 36th state to be added to the Union. Comstock Lode sparked the Silver Rush of 1859 and made Nevada the "Silver State." The Nevada Test Site was the first nuclear field test conducted with live troops

Attractions

Nevada is a state full of natural wonders, picturesque highways, little villages, and amazing outdoor activities. Recreational activities, including hiking, biking, climbing, horseback riding, and fishing, can be enjoyed at national parks and recreation zones.
